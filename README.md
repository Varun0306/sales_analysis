# sales_analysis
The file is a data visualization dashboard titled “MADHAV ECOMMERCE SALES DASHBOARD.” It presents key performance indicators and charts summarizing e-commerce sales activity. The design uses a dark purple background with bright, contrasting chart colors for readability.
Quarter Selection: Buttons for Qtr 1–4 and a dropdown for filtering by state.

Summary Metrics (Top):

121K Sum of AOV

37K Sum of Profit

5615 Sum of Quantity

438K Sum of Amount

Charts & Visuals:

Bar Chart: Sales amount by state (Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, Delhi, Gujarat, Andhra Pradesh, West Bengal).

Donut Chart: Quantity by category — Furniture (17%), Electronics (21%), Clothing (63%).

Bar Chart: Monthly profit trends (Jan–Dec) with positive and negative values.

Bar Chart: Sales amount by customer (Harivansh, Madhav, Madan Mohan, Shiva, Vishakha).

Donut Chart: Quantity by payment mode — COD (44%), UPI (21%), EMI (10%), Debit Card (13%), Credit Card (12%).

Horizontal Bar Chart: Profit by sub-category — Printers, Bookcases, Saree, Accessories, Tables.

Overall Purpose:
The dashboard provides a comprehensive snapshot of sales performance, highlighting revenue distribution, profit fluctuations, customer contributions, product categories, and payment preferences. It is designed for quick insights into business health and decision-making.
